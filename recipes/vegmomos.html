Veg Momos

Description

Making vegetarian momos with a spicy red chutney. Momos is one of the most popular dishes from Nepal and Tibet. These are also known as Dim Sum and are basically dumplings made from flour with a savory stuffing. Learn to make these popular Tibetan recipe of easy vegetable momos from scratch with two folding techniques. The recipe is vegan.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • Finely Chopped Veggies of your choice-(Cabbage, Carrots, French beans, Onions)
  • Oil, and Salt to taste

Steps

  1. Make the dough
  2. Add oil and garlic in the oil
  3. Put the finely chopped veggies with salt and pepper to taste
  4. Make small pieces of dough and roll them thin
  5. Add the stuffing to the dough in the center
  6. Steam or fry the dough with the stuffing
  7. grind green chillis, and tomatoes together with some garlic for serving the momos with a spicy chutney
