Sweet Potato Brownies

Description

Make a quick and healthy sweet potato brownie using the most basic ingredients.

Ingredients

  • Sweet Potato
  • Maple Syrup
  • Cocoa
  • Butter
  • Avocado Oil

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees.
  2. Make a potato puree by baking a sweet potato and mashing it in a bowl
  3. Add cocoa to the sweet potato along with maple syrup and butter and avocado oil
  4. Heat it in the oven.
  5. Add some mint and ice-cream on the side and tadaa!
